Tailored Pieces Ug clothing a line of custom-made clothing, made to appeal to those synonymous with the Tailored Pieces Ug Collection. At its initiation in 2019, the line was initiated with the release of the t-shirt line; kicked off with the barcoded line and later the Crested Crane line, in celebration of the revival of Air Uganda.

The clothing line was officially launched in 2019, by the brand’s proprietor Marion Nansibo, and is in stock at the Tailored Pieces Ug.
