Our Story

Welcome to Tailored Pieces Ug. We are a leader in both traditional African outfits and urban fashion styles in Makindye Luwafu. We offer both rental and custom outfits for all ages, both men and women.. You also will find dresses and accessories full of whimsy, femininity, and charm. Dresses are perfect for flower girls, photography, and so much more

February 20th, 2019 Tailored Pieces Ug was birthed.

Our 3 year span in the business has been quite an eventful, but fulfilling journey that has seen our portfolio grow from fashion, entertainment to the hospitality business.

Our approach Right from its foundation, Tailored Pieces Ug is set out to create a world-class service/product tailored for the modern and fashion-conscious consumer.

Our approach is to have class and exquisite taste at the forefront of many of our products, giving our clientele a once-in-a-lifetime and unforgettable experience.

Whether it’s a piece of clothing from our men’s apparel or the hospitality at any of our events, we strive to ensure that our guests/clients receive a world class product from our brand.

We thrive on our creativity and flexibility, a trait that enables us to cater to any of our clients that come our way.
